Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 3, 2026

Minesweeper – Find and Seek respects your privacy.

This app does not require users to create an account and does not directly collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, precise location, or payment information from users. However, this app uses third-party services to display advertisements and improve app reliability. These third-party services may collect, process, and share certain user data as described below.

Information Collection and Use

The app uses Google AdMob, a third-party advertising service provided by Google. Google AdMob may collect and share data including, but not limited to:

  • Device or other identifiers, such as advertising ID and app set ID
  • Device information, such as device type, operating system, and IP address
  • App interaction data, such as ad views, ad clicks, and app usage events
  • Diagnostics and app performance data, such as crash logs and performance information

This data may be used for advertising or marketing, analytics, fraud prevention, security, and compliance purposes.

Data Sharing

Data collected through Google AdMob may be transmitted to Google and its service providers. The app developer does not sell users' personal information and does not operate an independent user account database.

Data Security

Data transmitted by third-party services is transmitted using secure transport mechanisms where supported by those services. The app developer does not directly store advertising identifiers, app interaction data, or diagnostic data on a separate developer-operated server.

Third-Party Services

This app uses the following third-party service:

  • Google AdMob

User Choices

Users can reset or limit the use of their advertising ID through device settings. Depending on the device and Android version, users may also be able to delete or opt out of personalized advertising identifiers in the device privacy or ads settings.

Children's Information

This app is designed for a general audience and is not specifically directed at children under the age of 13.

The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that your child has provided personal information through this app, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.
