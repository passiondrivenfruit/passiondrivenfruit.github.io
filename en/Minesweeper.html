About

Minesweeper – Find and Seek is a classic puzzle game rebuilt for mobile. Find all hidden mines, place flags strategically, and clear the board without triggering a bomb.

How to Play

Tap a tile to reveal it. Numbers show how many mines touch that tile, including diagonals. Use those clues to mark mines with flags and open safe spaces.

Features

Enjoy clean controls, quick restarts, and multiple board sizes for both short sessions and longer strategy runs.

Tips

Start in the corners or edges to reduce guesswork. Look for "1" tiles that sit next to a single closed square, then chain your discoveries to open bigger safe areas.
